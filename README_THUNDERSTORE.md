## üéØ The Quest: "Spread the Word"

Talk to Big Willy to start his quest. He needs your help spreading the "Stay Silly" message by tagging graffiti spots around town - just like promoting his YouTube channel!

### Quest Objectives:

1. Talk to Big Willy to accept the quest
2. Tag between 1-5 graffiti spots around town (adapts based on available surfaces)
3. Receive text message updates on your progress
4. Return to Big Willy to claim your reward

### Quest Features:

- **Dynamic Requirements**: Quest adapts to available graffiti spots (1-5 tags required)
- **POI Markers**: Quest markers guide you to the nearest untagged spray surface
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time counter shows how many spots you've tagged
- **Text Updates**: Big Willy texts you with encouraging messages as you progress
- **Save Integration**: Quest progress is fully saved and persists across sessions

### Reward:

Complete the quest to receive the exclusive **Stay Silly Cap** - a custom wearable clothing item that you can't get anywhere else!

## üì¶ Installation

1. Download the latest **BigWillyMod** package from Thunderstore
2. Extract to your game's `BepInEx/plugins` or `Mods` folder (depending on your mod loader)
3. Ensure **S1API** is installed (required dependency)
4. Launch the game - Big Willy will spawn automatically!

## üéÆ How to Play

1. Launch the game and load your save (or start a new game)
2. Find Big Willy following his daily schedule (check the locations above)
3. Talk to Big Willy to start the "Spread the Word" quest
4. Follow the quest markers to graffiti spots around town
5. Tag the required number of spots with spray paint
6. Return to Big Willy to complete the quest and claim your Stay Silly Cap!

### Console Commands (Optional):

```
give stay_silly_cap 1    # Spawn the Stay Silly Cap directly in your inventory
```

## üîß Technical Details

This mod showcases advanced S1API features:

- **NPC System**: Custom NPC with full schedule, appearance, and AI behavior
- **Quest System**: Dynamic quest with progress tracking and conditional dialogue
- **Dialogue System**: Context-aware dialogue trees with state management
- **Graffiti Integration**: Hooks into the game's graffiti system via Harmony patches
